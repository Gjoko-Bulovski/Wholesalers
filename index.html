About Us

Wholesalers is a private company founded in 2000. The company's activity is Wholesalers with pharmaceutical products, medical devices, dietary supplements, cosmetic and chemical products and other products from the range necessary for pharmacy.

Quality policy

Wholesalers is a private company founded in 2000. The company's activity is wholesalers with pharmaceutical products, medical devices, dietary supplements, cosmetic and chemical products and other products from the range necessary for pharmacy. Mission of Wholesalers is the provision of services to supply on the wholesalers market (import, purchase, storage and distribution of pharmaceutical products, medical devices, dietary supplements, cosmetic products and other products from the pharmacy range) that meet the requirements of the users and the requirements of the relevant regulations and in the part of the companies for which wholesalers is the agencies providing services related to the regulation and maintenance of the pharmacovigilance system.Wholesalers aims to: • Provide quality services to its customers on the domestic market with the competence and professionalism of employees creating added value for users, employees, and society; • provide and cover a significant part of the needs of the domestic market • constantly increases the market in which it provides its services. We will achieve the goal by implementing and improving the quality management system in line with the requirements of ISO 9001: 2015, which includes: and managing the precession that leads to the creation of satisfied users and implementation of measures to improve customer satisfaction; • introducing leadership as one of the principles of governance; • active participation of the employees in the planning, implementation and evaluation of the activities in the averages of the realization of the services; • managing processes and resources for implementing, maintaining and continuously improving the quality management system; • improving the quality of services and products delivered; • identification of corrective measures and identification of the risks and possibilities for continuous improvement of the quality of the products and services offered • to decide on the basis of collected records and the analyzed data; • developing partnerships with all stakeholders; • Continuous modernization of the equipment and training of the employees; • We provide long-term and quality cooperation with our clients; • suppliers and partners in order to provide quality products and services.

Positioning

By putting users of services at the center of their interest Wholesalers has won the trust of many foreign drug manufacturers by recognizing Wholesaler as a reliable partner that provides quality and continuous supply to its customers and the ability to quickly respond to market demands and changes.
